# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in any byphantom repository or the byphantom.ai live product, please report it privately to:

**amit.chawla@reward360.co**

Please include:

- A description of the vulnerability and its potential impact
- Steps to reproduce
- Any proof-of-concept code or screenshots
- Your contact info so we can follow up

## What to expect

- Acknowledgement within 48 hours
- Triage and initial assessment within 5 business days
- Coordinated disclosure on a timeline that prioritizes member safety (we serve HDFC Bank's UHNI / HNI cardholders — production impact decisions go through Head of Concierge)
- Credit in fix-release notes (with your permission) for responsible disclosure

## Out of scope

- Reports requiring physical access to a user's device
- Social engineering of byphantom employees
- DoS / volumetric attacks against byphantom.ai
- Findings already mitigated by Cloudflare / Vercel / Supabase platform defenses

## Confidentiality

Please do NOT disclose the vulnerability publicly or to third parties until we've confirmed it's fixed and coordinated a release window with you.

Thank you for helping keep PHANTOM safe.
